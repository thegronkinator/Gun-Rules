Semi-Auto:
Semi auto is the traditional reloading mechanic for crossbows, and other traditional dnd weapons,
Except that you only need to reload when your mag is emptied.

Ammo types:
Different ammo goes to different guns, and is measured in different ways.

Light ammo:
For weapons classified as light, be it one-handed, or lightweight but two-handed.

Medium:
For medium sized guns

Heavy:
For heavy weaponry

Darts:
For more old fashioned equipment

Shells:
For shotguns, and similar sorts of scattered weapons.

Magnet rounds:
For inserting in magnetic weapons.

Magic ammo:
For magic weapons only, rather than reloading tangible ammo, roll DC15 with your spellcasting modifier to reload.

Canisters:
For air compressor weapons.
Gun tiers:
Guns in gracile are ranked by how effective they are, and thus, which you should give to who, you don't give a toddler a rocket launcher.

Tier 1: purchasable at levels 1-5

Tier 2: purchasable at levels 6-10

Tier 3: purchasable at levels 11-15

Tier 4: purchasable at levels 16-20
Misfire:
Upon rolling a nat1 while attacking with a gun, your gun immediately ceases to function, this can be remedied with a successful dexterity(tinkers tools) check, depending on the weapon, misfire may carry extra consequences.
